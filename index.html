Spiral 1

Independent Research Collective & Data Systems

Bridging dry-lab computational innovation with translational medical frameworks. We specialize in multi-omics data integration, single-cell transcriptomics, and macromolecular dynamics simulations.

About the Collective

Spiral 1 operates as a self-funded, highly agile virtual research collective. By leveraging high-performance cloud environments and open-source development frameworks, we decouple high-impact scientific inquiry from traditional structural boundaries.

Research Focus Clusters

01 / BioMedical

Single-cell and spatial transcriptomics integration, multi-omics data mining, and machine learning pipelines for predictive disease biomarkers.

02 / Technologies

Open-source bioinformatic package engineering, custom Bash/R/Python pipelines, and scalable architectures for handling massive next-generation sequencing datasets.

03 / Therapeutics

Structure-based computational drug discovery, ligand-protein interactions, and high-throughput atomistic molecular dynamics simulations.

Ethics & Institutional Disclosure

Commitment to Scientific Transparency

Spiral 1 is an independent, decentralized virtual research organization based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. As a self-funded entity, we operate without direct institutional state grants, ensuring complete intellectual flexibility. We adhere strictly to the COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines. All computational allocations, high-performance computing (HPC) workflows, and molecular simulations associated with our publications are provisioned and audited independently.
